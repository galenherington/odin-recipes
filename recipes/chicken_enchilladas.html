Chicken Recipe

    Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • Cumin
  • Chili Powder
  • Tortillas
    Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1-1/2 cups reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 1-1/2 cups reduced-fat sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
      • Instructions
    1. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, chili powder, garlic salt and cumin. Place 1/3 cup chicken mixture down the center of each tortilla. Roll up and place seam side down in two 13x9-in. baking dishes coated with cooking spray.
    2. In a large saucepan, whisk flour and broth until smooth. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Reduce heat; stir in sour cream and Monterey Jack cheese until melted.
    3. Pour sauce over enchiladas; sprinkle with Mexican cheese blend.
    4. Bake, uncovered, at 350Â° until bubbly, 15-20 minutes. Top with tomatoes if desired.
