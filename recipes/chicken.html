Chicken Recipe

    Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • Sour Cream
  • Onion
  • Tortillas
  • Monteray Jack Cheese
    Instructions
  1. Saute the onion
  2. Mix sour cream and cheese
  3. Mix cooked chicken and onion
  4. Place chicken and onion in Tortillas and roll
  5. Cover with sour cream mixture/li>
  6. Bake at 375 for 30 minutes
