Butter Swim Biscuits

    Ingredients
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 3/4 stick butter
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 4 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
    Instructions
  1. Combine the dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Stir in the buttermilk
  3. Melt butter in pan
  4. Pour mixture over the butter. Do not stir.
  5. Cook at 450° for 20-25 minutes
