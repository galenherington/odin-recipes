King Ranch Chicken

Ingredients

  • Shredded Chicken
  • 2 Cans of Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 Can of Rotel
  • 2 Cups of Cheddar Cheese
  • Tortillas
  • Sauteed Onions
    Directions
  • Combine chicken, soup, rotel, and onions in a bowl
  • Tear tortillas and place in bottom of greased pan then cover with chicken mixture and cheese
  • Repeat above step and then cook at 375° for 45 minutes
